Commercial Services

We support projects, programmes and organisations so that teams become better together.

Constructive Collaboration Session Sequence

The Constructive Collaboration process and software has been developed and refined through deployment
on over 20 major construction projects. It is suitable
for any sector that strives to improve team work, particularly those that face more complex challenges that can only be solved by drawing in
the right people
to work as multi-disciplinary teams which involve
a variety of organisations.

The facilitated process enables teams to come together to form
a shared view of current working challenges.
A real time predictive model allows teams to visualise future impacts and supports the agreement of actions to help progressive improvement.

A regular review cycle is deployed to maintain elevated performance levels through all work
stages of project delivery, and to ensure that when new challenges arise, teams can work together to address further issues before they impact.

Our process is in continual development and our ethos
is to share knowledge, with all participants, about the broader lessons and assimilate findings about how collaboration works.
