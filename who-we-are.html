Who We Are

Our core team is an agile hub for the projects we support and the wider collaborative community.

Andrew Wright instigated and founded Constructive Collaboration in January 2015. He leads a core team that researches, designs, develops, and delivers services to our clients. We are growing our initial core team to create an exceptional agile collaborative team.

The core team draws on the input of a long standing network of specialists covering specialist software, legal services accountants and others.

We operate with the advice of our extended informal collaborative network that includes our clients, the delivery teams we support and the societal groups who help the enterprise.
