Our Story

We are on
a journey
of
true collaboration.

In 2015, Andrew Wright set
out on a quest to demystify collaboration, and work out
how we
can make great teams
the norm, not the exception.

Drawing on best practice
from around the globe,
and the commitment and energy of our network
and community, we developed the Constructive Collaboration offer, through
a sequence of
iterative research
and practice.

Today our process is supported
by bespoke software, being used
by leading clients across the construction industry, and out
core team is constantly developing this highly innovative process, and software as well as the analytic opportunities available.

Since July 2016 over 1,000 people in more than 100 organisations have used our process in practice and these numbers are steadily growing.
